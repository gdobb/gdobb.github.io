About us

Experience the Magic of Mt. Holly

Located in the heart of Southeast Michigan, Mt. Holly has been a winter destination for families and snow lovers for over 60 years. With 19 scenic trails, 7 chairlifts, and a full-service lodge, we’re proud to offer a fun, safe, and affordable outdoor experience for all ages and skill levels.

Lessons & Rentals
Learn to Ski or Ride – We’ve Got You Covered

New to the mountain? Sign up for private or group lessons with our certified instructors. We also offer full equipment rentals – skis, snowboards, boots, helmets – everything you need to hit the slopes with confidence.

Come visit!

Ski the East, Midwest Style
Mt. Holly is proud to stand with the Ski the East movement, showing that you don’t need the Rockies to ride hard and live loud. As one of Michigan’s most iconic local hills, we bring the energy, community, and passion that define East Coast grit—right here in the Midwest. Whether you’re carving your first turns or chasing powder all season long, Mt. Holly is where you earn your stripes and rep your region.
